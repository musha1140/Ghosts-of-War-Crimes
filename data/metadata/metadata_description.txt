Dataset Name: War Crimes Dataset
Description: This dataset records incidents of war crimes and crimes against humanity, including location, date, number of victims, and responsible parties.

Columns:
1. Incident Type: Type of war crime (e.g., bombing, chemical weapon use).
2. Date: Date of the incident in YYYY-MM-DD format.
3. Location: City or region where the incident occurred.
4. Number of Victims: Approximate number of victims.
5. Responsible Party: Entity or group responsible.
6. Latitude/Longitude: Coordinates for mapping.
7. Source: Verified source (e.g., UN, WHO).

Processing Notes:
- Cleaned for duplicates.
- Standardized locations to city-country format.
- Dates converted to ISO format.
